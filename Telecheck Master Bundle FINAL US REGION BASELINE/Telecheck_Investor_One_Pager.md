# Telecheck — Investor One-Pager

---

## The company

Telecheck is an AI-powered telehealth, pharmacy, and health intelligence platform that helps patients get assessed, treated, monitored, and guided through a complete digital care journey. It unifies telemedicine, pharmacy fulfillment, lab interpretation, remote monitoring, AI clinical assistance, and patient community into one connected system.

## The problem

Healthcare in emerging markets is fragmented, unsupported, and poorly fit for chronic disease. Telemedicine, pharmacy, labs, monitoring, and follow-up live in separate systems — the patient carries the integration burden. Medication history, lab trends, herbal co-medication, and monitoring data are never interpreted together. And Western-first telehealth products ignore the realities that define emerging-market care: counterfeit medication risk, herb-drug interactions, workforce constraints, and multi-generational family care.

## The solution

Telecheck is the platform that stitches everything together. One patient journey from symptom intake through AI triage, clinician review, prescription, pharmacy fulfillment, delivery, monitoring, and follow-up. AI interprets across the full clinical picture — labs, medications, herbal medicines, conditions, and behavior. Configurable governance enables safe operation across jurisdictions with different regulatory regimes, clinical protocols, and workforce realities.

## What makes Telecheck different

**Herb-drug interaction intelligence.** The only telehealth platform with phytochemical-aware interaction checking. 30-50 Ghana/West African herbal preparations at launch, with institutional partnership potential (Korle Bu Hospital, Ghana FDA).

**Five-class medication interaction engine.** Drug-drug, drug-condition, drug-lab, genotype-aware, and special clinical flags (hemoglobinopathy-aware dosing, pregnancy/lactation, pediatric/geriatric, polypharmacy) — running on every prescription and refill.

**Two-mode AI architecture.** Mode 1: patient-facing conversational assistant under admin-configurable guardrails. Mode 2: protocol execution agent for structured async care with physician sign-off. Both governed by an immutable safety floor.

**Fake medication detection.** Multi-method advisory detection (verification codes, visual inspection, batch validation, supply chain provenance, pharmacovigilance correlation) integrated into the pharmacy workflow.

**Market Rollout Cockpit.** Jurisdiction-aware governance state layer with structured Market Packs, dependency checking, blast-radius preview, evidence locker, and one-click rollback. Built for controlled multi-market expansion.

## Business model

Four revenue engines at launch, all patient-pay:

- **Consultation fees** — per-visit async and sync consults, price displayed upfront
- **Medication sales** — prescription and OTC through hybrid pharmacy (platform + partner), margin on every order
- **Frictionless refills** — medication-cost only, no consult fee; drives adherence and recurring volume
- **RPM/CCM subscriptions** — monthly chronic-care monitoring; recurring revenue

Acquisition engines (food scanning, fitness tracking, pregnancy tracking, AI assistant, community) are free — they feed clinical revenue, not direct monetization.

## Market

**Anchor market: Ghana.** 34.5M population, 70% internet penetration, 113% mobile connection rate. Real chronic-care demand (diabetes, hypertension), real herb-drug interaction demand, real counterfeit medication risk. Clinical partners: Korle Bu Hospital, Ghana FDA. Mobile money infrastructure established.

**Expansion path:** Ghana → West Africa (Nigeria, Kenya) → global including US. Architecture is global from day one — jurisdiction-aware governance, per-market protocol activation, cloud-hosted.

## Ghana launch posture

Mobile-first, low-friction patient acquisition led by tracking, self-assessment, GLP-1, and men's health programs. Conservative AI guardrails with staged protocol activation. Clinician-reviewed refill pathways with protocol-authorized renewal built but activated after regulatory sign-off. Hybrid pharmacy with delivery in Accra metro. RPM for diabetes management. Moderated patient community (3 launch groups). Advisory-only fake medication detection.
